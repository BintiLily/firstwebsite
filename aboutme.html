About Me

My name is Lilian and I was born in London and originally come from Tanzania.


Currently residing in Greater Manchester, where I have started my own family.


I am a Community Leader within the Tanzanian Community.


My educational background is in Media Production and my work experience has been in Compliance within the Logistics Industry.


After having my second child I moved to Swinton and I have been volunteering in a Woman's Charity leading different projects, my Community Role and I am currently trying to gain new skills by doing a sixteen week bootcamp for Data Analysis and Project Management for a career change; hence why I am here to learn coding which will assist with employment chances.


My Mother who is now retired, was a System Analyst and she taught me computers and created my email address back in the 90's that I still use to date. Back in the 90's my Mother did try to teach me programming but I just wasn't getting it. She is over the moon to hear I am coding again and I really do hope this will take me to new and exciting opportunities in the future.


About TAGMA

TAGMA  is a community based organisation that is constitution based.


TAGMA  has been in existence for over ten years and its presence is to reinforce its members socially and provide them with a sense of belonging.

Message from The United Republic of Tanzanian High Commissioner to the United Kingdom and Republic of Ireland

I wish to offer my warmest greetings to all Tanzanians living in the United Kingdom and Republic of Ireland, as well as friends of Tanzania and visitors of this website.


We have endeavored to make this website informative and user friendly, sharing information about Tanzania, trade and investment opportunities available in Tanzania, information on consular requirements, as well as activities of this High Commission in its pursuit of enhancing bilateral relations with the United Kingdom and Republic of Ireland.


I encourage all Tanzanian citizens and permanent residents in the United Kingdom and Republic of Ireland to register with the High Commission in order to be kept up to date with news, events and key information from Tanzania.


I thank you for visiting our  website  and also for your interest in Tanzania.


Tanzania Hakuna Matata.



H.E Dr. Asha-Rose Migiro

High Commissioner of the United Republic of Tanzania to the United Kingdom & Republic of Ireland.
